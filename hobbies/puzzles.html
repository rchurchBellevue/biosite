Puzzles

When the Covid-19 epidemic hit in 2020, a lot of people were stuck at home needing something to fill their time. Although Jeni had always enjoyed doing jigsaw puzzles as a child, she really embraced the activity during the pandemic and beyond.

For her, puzzles are a good way to keep her mind active and to provide a sense of accomplishment when one has been completed. Perhaps the only downfall is our cats seem to want to help with the puzzles which can make the process difficult.
